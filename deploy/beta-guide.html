Installer Management Platform

Welcome. This is a guided tour of the Armor Vue platform — a system built to manage window film installation jobs from the office all the way to the field. There are four roles, each with their own view. This page walks through all of them.

How an installer gets access:

  1. 1 Admin provisions the installer in the Admin Portal → Installers. A magic sign-in link is automatically emailed to them. No password is ever created. The link is their key.
  2. 2 Installer opens the email on their phone and taps the sign-in link. Should open in Safari or Chrome — not Gmail's built-in browser.
  3. 3 A confirmation screen appears. Installer taps Sign In to Portal →.
  4. 4 First login: draw a signature, complete a short tutorial. Portal is ready to use. Signature is stored and printed on all completed job records.
Magic links are one-time use. If an installer's link stops working, the admin can resend a fresh one from the ⟳ Link button in the Installers section.
